The IDR explained

What is an Interactive Digital Report?

An IDR is a fully immersive, 360° digital property report — unlike anything else in the UK.

Instead of a static PDF, an Interactive Digital Report lets buyers, lenders and agents explore a property room by room in full 360° — on any device, anywhere. Every IDR is captured on-site by a trained DigiSnapper, reviewed by a RICS-accredited surveyor, and delivered through the DigiProp platform. No other company in the UK provides this technology.

How it works

From instruction to report in 4 steps

Simple to use. Powerful results. Track every step live in your portal.

01

Instruct us

Log in to your DigiProp portal. Enter the property address and select your report type — IDR, RICS Level 2, or Level 3. Takes under 3 minutes.

02

We deploy

A trained DigiSnapper attends the property and captures a full 360° and 2D photographic record of every room, service and external area.

03

Survey review

A RICS-accredited surveyor reviews the complete capture using our AI-assisted report writing tool and produces the client's report.

04

IDR delivered

Your Interactive Digital Report lands in the portal — immersive, 360°, and shareable with buyers, lenders and clients instantly.
